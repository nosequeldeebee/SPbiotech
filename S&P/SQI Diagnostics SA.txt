SQI Diagnostics Inc. (TSXV:SQD) 2022 Form Doc
Business Description: University Health Network engages in providing patient care, education, and research services in Canada. It operates four hospitals, such as the Princess Margaret Cancer Centre, the Toronto General Hospital, the Toronto Western Hospital, and the Toronto Rehabilitation Institute. The company’s Princess Margaret Cancer Centre offers diagnostic, treatment, and follow-up care services in medical, surgical, and radiation oncology areas. Its Toronto General Hospital provides services in the areas of cardiac care, organ transplants, and treatment of complex patient needs. The company’s Toronto Western Hospital offers services in the areas of neuroscience, orthopaedics, rheumatology, musculoskeletal health, and arthritis, as well as surgery, critical care, and medical and community care programs. The company also operates the Michener Institute of Education at UHN, a post-secondary institution that provides health sciences education; OpenLab, a design and innovation shop for health care solutions; and rehabilitation centers. In addition, it provides catering, conference and educational technology, visual services, such as graphic design, photography, videography, and printing services, and vendor services. University Health Network was formerly known as The Toronto Hospital and changed its name to University Health Network in April 1999 The company was founded in 1986 and is based in Toronto, Canada.
