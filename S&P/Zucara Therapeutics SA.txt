Business Description: The Centre for Drug Research and Development operates as a drug development and commercialization company that provides expertise and infrastructure to enable researchers from various health research institutions to advance early-stage drug candidates in Canada. The company focuses on de-risking the discoveries stemming from publicly-funded health research and transforming them into investment opportunities for the private sector, as well as bridging the commercialization gap between early-stage academic research and industry. It provides expertise and infrastructure to source, evaluate, develop, and commercialize small molecule and biologic technologies in various therapeutic areas. The company also offers various drug development training programs for researchers. The Centre for Drug Research and Development has strategic collaborations with GlaxoSmithKline, F. Hoffmann-La Roche Ltd., and Pfizer. Centre for Drug Research and Development was founded in 2007 and is based in Vancouver, Canada.
