Business Description
IPS Therapeutique Inc. operates as a contract research organization which offers preclinical evaluation of new cardiovascular drugs. The company provides pre-clinical cardiovascular studies, cardiovascular efficacy and safety assessment, action potential duration analysis, and cardiac muscle tension measurement services. It caters to biopharmaceutical and nutraceutical companies. IPS Therapeutique Inc. was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Sherbrooke, Canada.
