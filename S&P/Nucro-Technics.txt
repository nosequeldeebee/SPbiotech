Business Description
Nucro-Technics, Inc. provides pharmaceutical, biologic, and medical device. It offers analytical chemistry, microbiology, toxicology, bioanalytical, genetic toxicology, stability testing, bioassay studies, QA/AC consulting, regulatory affairs, and logistics services. The company also provides medical device testing services, such as cytotoxicity, physicochemical tests, and infrared identification for raw material characterization and screening, acute and chronic toxicology, hemocompatibility studies, and mutagenicity testing for material biocompatibility testing, microbiological evaluation, sterilization cycle validation, and LAL/particulate analysis for product and process validation, and finished product qualification, release test, and audit services for routine testing. In addition, it offers natural health product services, including submission process management and coordination, needs assessment, regulatory submissions Preparation or pre-screening, ongoing quality assurance support, and testing programs design and management services. The company also serves cosmetic, medical device, chemical, specialty chemicals, personal care, household and hospital products, and petroleum industries, as well as government agencies. Nucro-Technics, Inc. was founded in 1970 and is headquartered in Scarborough, Canada. As of August 15, 2023, Nucro-Technics, Inc. operates as subsidiary of Frontage Canada, Inc.
