Business Description
Solabs, Inc. provides Web-based electronic quality management solutions in the United States and Canada. It offers solutions for quality processes tracking and monitoring, document management and workflow, and training records management. The company provides its solutions in software as a service model. It also offers technical support and training services. The company was founded in 1999 and is based in Montreal, Canada.
