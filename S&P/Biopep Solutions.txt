Business Description
Biopep Solutions Inc. is a development-stage biotechnology company that discovers and develops therapeutic products for the treatment of cancers and other diseases. The company develops BPS-001, a heterogeneous biologic extract containing a composition of multiple peptide and protein components. The company is based in Richmond, Canada.
