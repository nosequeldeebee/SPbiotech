Business Description
Soundbite Medical Solutions Inc. operates as a cardiovascular medical technology company. It engages in developing and commercializing proprietary shockwave device and generator technology delivered within the cardiovascular system to treat chronic total occlusions. The company was founded in 2013 and is based in Montreal, Canada.
