Business Description: Science Exchange, Inc. operates an online marketplace for scientific services to collaborate with academic and government laboratories. Its platform assists researchers in searching scientific services from the U.S. research institutions; and researchers to compare service pricing, filter through quality testimonials, and pay for the service. The company also provides an enterprise dynamic report tool that enables pharma and biotech companies to perform real-time tracking of key performance indicators related to their outsourced R&D investments. Science Exchange, Inc. was incorporated in 2011 and is based in San Francisco, California.
