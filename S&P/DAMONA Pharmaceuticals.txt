Business Description
Damona Pharmaceuticals Inc. offers drug development and therapeutics with molecules that target and restore impaired brain functions in patients suffering from cognitive symptoms. The company provides modulators of GABA neurotransmitters that target and restore impaired brain functions in patients suffering from cognitive symptoms associated with depression and diseases of aging. Damona Pharmaceuticals Inc. was formerly known as Alpha Cog Inc. The company was incorporated in 2019 and is based in Toronto, Canada.
