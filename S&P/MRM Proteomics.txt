Business Description
MRM Proteomics Inc. operates as a contract research organization (CRO) which provides protein quantification and proteomic services. The company offers PeptiQuant Protein Quantification Assay, for quantification of proteins and biomarkers in a single sample; PeptiQuant Human Discovery MRM Assay, for human protein quantification and biomarker discovery; PeptiQuant Mouse Discovery MRM Assay, a 34 mouse protein multiplex assay; iTRAQ Protein Quantification, for protein quantification in complex mixture by mass spectrometry; and Structural Proteomics, for determining protein tertiary structure and protein-ligand interactions. Additionally, it provides SIS (stable isotope-labeled) peptides for MRM (multiple reaction monitoring) mass spectrometry analysis. The company caters to pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and diagnostic industries. MRM Proteomics Inc. was founded in 2010 and is based in Vancouver, Canada.
