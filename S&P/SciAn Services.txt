Business Description
SciAn Services, Inc., a contract research organization, provides clinical development services to pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device enterprises in North America. It offers strategic consulting, trial management, and data management services. The company also provides study design and protocol development, trial and site management, data management, electronic data capture, biostatistics, clinical research reports and manuscripts, consulting, regulatory support/SAE reporting, and product safety monitoring services. SciAn Services, Inc. was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Toronto, Canada with additional offices in the United States.
