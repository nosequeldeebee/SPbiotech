Trine Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (Out of Business)
Biotechnology
Trine Pharmaceuticals, Inc. 2003 Form 10-K
Business Description: As of March 31, 2008, Trine Pharmaceuticals, Inc. went out of business. Trine Pharmaceuticals Inc. is a biopharmaceutical company that develops and markets pharmaceutical products for gastrointestinal and metabolic diseases. The company also conducts pre-clinical and clinical testing of wound-healing compounds and focuses on antibiotic and antifungal resistance. It Cephalosporin program, in collaboration with Johnson & Johnson, targets serious gram-positive multidrug resistant infections. In addition, the company's Efflux Pump Inhibitor Program, targets gram-negative infections. It was formerly known as Essential Therapeutics Inc. and changed its name to Trine Pharmaceuticals, Inc. to September 23, 2004. Trine Pharmaceuticals was founded in 1992 and is based in Waltham, Massachusetts.
