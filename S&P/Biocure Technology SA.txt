Key DevelopmentStrategic Alliances Jan-14-2019
Business Description: Y-Biologics Inc. engages in the research and development of immuno-oncology drugs and antibody drugs. It develops YBL-006, a PD-1 immune checkpoint inhibitor that is in clinical phase 1/2a medication for solid cancers. The company also develops mono-specific antibodies, such as YBL-011 and YBL-003 for solid cancers; YBL-013, an ALiCE antibody for solid cancers; and YBL-001, an anti-drug conjugate for small cell lung and liver cancers. Y-Biologics Inc. was incorporated in 2007 and is based in Daejeon, South Korea.
