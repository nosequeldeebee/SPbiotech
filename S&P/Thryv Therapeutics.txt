Business Description
Thryv Therapeutics Inc., a biopharmaceutical company, discovers and develops therapies for the treatment of long QT syndrome. The company offers SGK-1, a regulator of the sodium channel in heart cells and over-activity of kinase recapitulates long QT syndrome and is associated with arrhythmias. Thryv Therapeutics Inc. was formerly known as LQT Therapeutics, Inc and changed its name to Thryv Therapeutics Inc. in July 2022. The company was incorporated in 2019 and is based in Laval, Canada.
