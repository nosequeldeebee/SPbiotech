Business Description: International Centre For Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology engages in the research and training of molecular biology and biotechnology. The company holds knowledge and applies techniques in the areas of biomedicine, crop improvement, environmental protection/remediation, and biopharmaceuticals and biopesticide production.
