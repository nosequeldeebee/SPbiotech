Business Description
O'hara Technologies Inc. designs and fabricates a line of pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and confectionary equipment for coating, mixing, blending, granulating, and drying. The company offers tablet coating products, which comprise laboratory, pilot, production, and continuous/batch coating systems; and fluid bed dryers granulators, including laboratory multi-purpose fluid bed systems, pilot fluid bed dryers/granulators, production fluid bed dryers/granulators, and production fluid bed multi processors. It also provides tray dry ovens, such as tray dry oven inserts and production tray dry ovens; and conventional coating systems, which comprise laboratory scale conventional coating pans, pilot scale conventional pans, production scale conventional pans, multiple pan conventional coating systems, and solution preparation products. In addition, the company offers V-shell and conical blenders, such as laboratory scale blenders, production tumble V-shell blenders, and production tumble conical blenders; material handling items; and system components and accessories, which comprise wash in place systems, spray systems, control systems, air handling units, and dust collection products. Further, it provides various services, including installation and start-up, preventive maintenance, site equipment audits, operator training, up-grades and adds-on, certified part replacement, and repair and/or exchange, as well as IQ, OQ, and calibration services. The company was founded in 1976 and is headquartered in Richmond Hill, Canada.
