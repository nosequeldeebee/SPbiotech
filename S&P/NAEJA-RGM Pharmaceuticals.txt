Business Description
NAEJA Pharmaceutical Inc., a pharmaceutical contract research and development company, specializes in pre-clinical discovery and development services. Its drug discovery solutions include compound library, early hits validation, leads optimization, evaluation of structure-activity relationships, and patent writing services. The company also provides early drug development solutions, including route identification, selection and validation, and synthesis and scaling up of reference and novel molecules. NAEJA Pharmaceutical Inc. was founded in 1999 and is based in Edmonton, Canada.
