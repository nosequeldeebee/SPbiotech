Business Description
MetaOptima Technology Inc. designs and builds gadgets, and develops mobile and Web e-health solutions. It offers MoleScope, a telemedicine solution for the prevention and detection of skin cancer. The company’s MoleScope enables users to check moles for signs of melanoma and skin cancer, take 3D/2D images of moles, and share pictures with dermatologist for an online skin cancer screening from home. Its MoleScope device can be attached to iPhone and Android smartphones. The company was incorporated in 2012 and is based in Vancouver, Canada.
