Business Description
Spiderwort develops biomaterials for regenerative medicine. The company's product vary from biological and synthetic polymers which are available in deifferent forms such as powders, gels, membrances and pastes. Spiderwort was founded in 2015 and is based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.
