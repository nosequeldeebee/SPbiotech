Business Description
Trulife Group Limited manufactures, markets, and distributes prosthetic and orthopedic products. It offers orthotics products, such as cervical, upper extremity, spinal, hip, knee, ankle foot orthoses, foot orthotics, pediatric care, platinum, and platinum elite products; and prosthetics products, including feet, knees, hip, modular, pediatric prosthetic, and endo and exoskeletal components, as well as locks and valves. The company also provides women’s health products, such as breast forms, bras or lingerie, and accessories; operating room products; and wheelchair cushions, covers, and accessories. In addition, it provides design and engineering services for customers; and engages in the creation, development, manufacture and marketing of aerospace, automotive, musical, and other commercial products. The company offers its products through its direct sales forces primarily in Ireland, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Canada, as well as through distributor partners internationally. Trulife Group Limited was founded in 1959 and is based in Dublin, Ireland with manufacturing sites worldwide.

Trulife Inc.
Trulife Inc. manufactures and distributes medical products in North America and internationally. It offers women’s healthcare products, including products for maternity and osteoporosis, lactation devices, and products for women who had surgery for breast cancer, as well as bras, breast forms, swimwear, and accessories for the post-mastectomy woman. The company also supplies orthopedic products, which consist of soft goods and rigid supports for the spine and extremities; and pressure care products, including products designed to treat venous and lymphatic insufficiencies, as well as seating products for the prevention of decubitus ulcers. In addition, it offers prosthetic supplies, such as accessories for the amputee, including stump socks, sheaths, shrinkers, and suspension sleeves. The company was formerly known as Camp Healthcare Inc. The company was founded in 1958 and is based in Jackson, Michigan with additional offices in Atlanta, Georgia Jackson, Michigan and Trenton, Ontario. Trulife Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Trulife Group Ltd.
