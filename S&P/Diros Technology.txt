Business Description
Diros Technology Inc. manufactures and sells radiofrequency products for neurosurgery and chronic pain management. The company was incorporated in 1983 and is based in Markham, Canada. As of July 24, 2023, Diros Technology Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Avanos Medical, Inc.
