Business Description
Bold Therapeutics Inc operates as a clinical-stage biotechnology company that develops cancer therapy for the treatment of gastric, pancreatic, colorectal, and other anti-cancer therapies. The company's products provide BOLD-100, a ruthenium-based small molecule therapeutic. Bold Therapeutics Inc was incorporated in 2018 and is based in Vancouver, Canada.
