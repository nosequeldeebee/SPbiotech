Business Description
EBT Medical, Inc. manufactures novel, a non-invasive neuromodulation therapy and associated ecosystem for patients with overactive bladder centered on the Saphenous nerve for pelvic floor disorders. The company was founded in 2014 and is based in Boston, Massachusetts with additional office in Toronto, Canada.
