Business Description
Mediphage Bioceuticals, Inc. develops a non-viral gene therapy platform. The company's platform is based on ministering DNA technology that allows for safe and revocable genetic medicines, enabling medical researchers to overcome the limitations of current viral vector-based gene therapy technologies. The company was incorporated in 2016 and is based in Toronto, Canada.
