Business Description
MIS Implants Technologies Ltd. engages in development and production of products and solutions for implant dentistry. Its portfolio includes conical connection implants, internal hexagon connection implants, one-piece mono-block implants, and orthodontic kits/screws/tools; prosthetic options, such as impression coping, temporary restoration, screw retained restoration, cemented restoration, and overdenture restoration; regenerative solutions, including bone grafting substitutes and barrier membranes; and digital dentistry solutions. The company also conducts online dental webinars; and provides international courses on basic and advanced implant placement and restoration, digital dentistry, and other topics through its education centers worldwide. It serves clinicians through a network of distributors worldwide. The company was founded in 1995 and is based in Shlomi, Israel. As of September 20, 2016, MIS Implants Technologies Ltd. operates as a subsidiary of DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.
