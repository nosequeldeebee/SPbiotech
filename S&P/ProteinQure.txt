Business Description
ProteinQure Inc. develops and manufactures protein-based therapeutic drugs. The company leverages quantum computing, molecular simulations, and reinforcement learning to develop novel therapeutics. The company provides structure determination, protein design, and property determination to enable drug discovery and design. ProteinQure Inc. was incorporated in 2017 and is based in Toronto, Canada.
