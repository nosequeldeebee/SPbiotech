Business Description
Axiom Real-Time Metrics Inc. develops clinical trial software. The company's software has end-user-focused, unified functionality and modules for entire clinical study data and operational needs, including electronic data capture (EDC), clinical trial management systems (CTMS), randomization (IWRS), and electronic patient-reported outcomes (ePRO). The company serves biotech companies, pharmaceutical companies, and clinical research organizations. The company was founded in 2001 and is based in Mississauga, Canada.
