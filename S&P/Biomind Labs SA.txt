Biomind Labs Inc. (OTCPK:BMND.F) 2023 Form Doc
Business Description: Hadasit Medical Research Services and Development Ltd., a technology transfer company, identifies, protects, nurtures, and commercializes inventions developed in Hadassah. The company assists physicians and researchers in defining the medical unmet needs, aligning the inventions and supporting research, and raising funds to support proof-of-concept development. It also offers pre-clinical services, including cell biology clinical diagnostics, consultations and counseling, disease models and experimental surgery, drug discovery, genetics, GMP production, and imaging. In addition, the company offers clinical research services, such as clinical trials contracts; budgeting and billing services; matching the investigators with sponsored projects through a database of investigators; available database of certified clinical research coordinators; and supporting Hadassah medical staff to improve the quality and effectiveness of research efforts. It serves pharmaceuticals, molecular diagnostics, and medical devices sectors. The company was incorporated in 1986 and is based in Jerusalem, Israel. Hadasit Medical Research Services and Development Ltd. operates as a subsidiary of Hadassah Medical Organisation (HMO).
