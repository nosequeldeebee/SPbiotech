Business Description
ExCellThera Inc. operates as a clinical-stage biotechnology company that develops molecules and bioengineering solutions to expand stem and immune cells for patients with hematologic malignancies. It also develops ECT-001 cell therapy, a cryo-preserved technology for allogeneic stem cell transplant. The company was incorporated in 2014 and is based in Montreal, Canada.
