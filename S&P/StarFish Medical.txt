Business Description
StarFish Product Engineering Inc. engages in designing, developing, and manufacturing medical devices for entrepreneurial medical device companies in North America. It offers desktop, handheld, diagnostic, therapeutic, and portable ophthalmic devices, as well as lab automation and capital equipment, and robotics. The company provides medical devices in the areas of cardiology, dentistry, emergency medicine, genetics, gynecology and obstetrics, hematology, microbiology, musculoskeletal, nephrology and urology, neuroscience, nuclear medicine, occupational medicine, oncology, ophthalmology, orthopedics, pediatrics, radiology, rehabilitation, and surgery. It also offers devices in the areas of ultrasound and acoustics, FPGA, optics and spectroscopy, magnetics, microprocessors and firmware, user interface development, contextual and interaction research, engineering physics, precision mechanism and mechanical design, control systems, fluid dynamics and micro-fluidics, software application development and databases, analog electronics, light therapy, industrial design, plastics design, disposables, high voltage, lasers, and radiation. In addition, the company’s services include conceptual development, technology optimization, product development, quality management, regulatory support, manufacturing, and sustaining engineering services. StarFish Product Engineering Inc. was founded in 1994 and is based in Victoria, Canada.
