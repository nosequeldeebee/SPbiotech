Business Description: National Research Council Canada is an institution that undertake, promotes, and assists in scientific and industrial research. It offers services, such as maintaining and operating a national science library, publishing and selling scientific and technical information, working on the standardization and certification of scientific and technical apparatus and instruments and materials used, and operating and administering any astronomical observatories established or maintained by the Government of Canada. National Research Council Canada was founded in 1916 and is based in Ottawa, Canada.
