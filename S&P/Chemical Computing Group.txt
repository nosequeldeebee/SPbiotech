Business Description
Chemical Computing Group ULC operates as a software company that provides drug discovery applications. It offers Molecular Operating Environment, an integrated drug discovery software package; and PSILO, a structure database and visualization system that provides a consolidated repository for macromolecular and protein-ligand structural information. The company was founded in 1994 and is based in Montreal, Canada with additional offices in Tokyo, Japan; Paris, France; Cologne, Germany; and Cambridge, United Kingdom.
