Business Description
Inspire Biotherapeutics Inc., a biotechnology company, develops gene therapies for monogenic and acquired diseases of the lung. The company develops a lung tropic AAV gene therapy platform that demonstrates transduction and long-term expression in lung tissue without impact on other tissue types in disease-relevant animal models. The company’s platform aims to treat cystic fibrosis, interstitial lung disease, pulmonary fibrosis, and other monogenic lung diseases. The company was incorporated in 2020 and is based in Toronto, Canada.
